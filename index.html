Who We Are

Peace Lutheran is a congregation in the Lutheran tradition, committed to proclaiming Christ crucified and risen through the faithful preaching of His Word and the administration of His Sacraments. As a congregation of The Lutheran Church—Missouri Synod, we confess the Holy Scriptures as the inspired and inerrant Word of God and uphold the Lutheran Confessions as a true and faithful exposition of that Word.

Quick Beliefs

Scripture

We confess the Holy Scriptures of the Old and New Testaments as the inspired and inerrant Word of God and the only rule and norm of faith and life.

Sacraments

We confess that Christ gives forgiveness, life, and salvation through His Sacraments—Holy Baptism and the Lord's Supper—where He truly and graciously delivers what He promises.

Grace

We confess that we are saved by grace alone through faith alone in Christ alone, not by our works, but by His mercy won for us in His death and resurrection.
