Christian Yoga

Thursdays • 7:00 PM — Lower Level • No cost, no registration

Why We Use Guided Meditation

At the end of our Christian yoga class, we incorporate guided meditation during Savasana (Corpse Pose) to create a sacred space for deep rest, reflection, and connection with God. After engaging our bodies in movement, we intentionally quiet our minds and hearts, focusing on Scripture and God’s presence. This time of stillness allows us to fully surrender our worries, release tension, and receive the peace of Christ.

A Christ-centered Practice

Unlike secular meditation, our practice is rooted in God’s Word, directing our thoughts toward His truth, His promises, and His sustaining grace. As we rest, we reflect on Scripture, breathe deeply in His presence, and trust in His provision—allowing our faith to be nourished just as our bodies have been strengthened.

Join Us

Whether after yoga or in any quiet moment, this guided meditation offers a Christ-centered way to rest, renew, and restore your soul. The class is beginner-friendly (Hatha) and meets each Thursday evening. No cost, no registration — bring a mat and a heart ready to be still.

Scripture & Practice

We weave Scripture into practice — breathing with awareness, moving in asana, and closing in meditation. Psalm 46:10 (ESV): “Be still, and know that I am God…”

Breath

Each practice begins with a few minutes of focused attention on our breath; inhaling and exhaling fully. Job 33:4 (ESV): “The Spirit of God has made me, and the breath of the Almighty gives me life.” In the Bible the word for breath is the same word as Spirit — a fitting reminder that our breathing practice connects us to God’s life within us.

Asana

Asana means "pose." We use gentle Hatha sequences (Savasana, Bharmanasana, standing and seated poses) to strengthen, lengthen, and release tension. Flexibility grows with practice — you don't need to be "flexible" to begin.

Meditation

After movement and breath we settle into meditation — usually in Savasana — listening to Scripture, receiving God's peace, and sitting quietly with the Lord. Exodus 14:14 (ESV): “The LORD will fight for you, and you have only to be silent.”

Teacher

David Balla — Registered Yoga Teacher (RYT 200) with Yoga Alliance and pastor of Peace Lutheran Church. David blends theological grounding with gentle, accessible movement and invites prayerful reflection in every class.
